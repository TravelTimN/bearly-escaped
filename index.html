HOW WE USE COOKIES

Like many websites, we use cookies to store and retrieve small bits of information on your computer when you visit.
This information is used to make the site work as you expect it to work.
It is not personally identifiable to you, but it can be used to give you a more personalised web experience.

Some of the information stored is put there by other companies, whose software we have added to the site, and this can also impact your experience of other websites that you may visit after leaving our site.

If you continue to use this site without taking action to prevent the storage of this information, you are effectively agreeing to this use.
If you want to learn more about the general uses of cookies, including how to stop them being stored by your computer, please visit Cookiepedia – all about cookies.

Below is a list of the different types of cookies used on this site, and an explanation of what they are used for.
If you would like any more information, please get in touch with us directly.

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function.
They are usually only set in response to actions made by you which amount to a request for services, such as logging in or filling in forms.

You can set your browser to block or alert you about these cookies, but some parts of the site will no longer work.
These cookies do not store any personally identifiable information.

About Us

Bearly Escaped will be Dublin’s newest, and most unique, live escape game experience. Designed to provide interactive fun and excitement, our rooms are ideal for playing with your friends, family, or colleagues.

So, what exactly are Escape Rooms, and how do they work?

Are you adventurous and creative?
Do you love challenges and work well in a team?
Perhaps you’re curious and enjoy a mystery?
Whatever your personality, there’s something for everyone at Bearly Escaped!

Using everyone’s skills, intellect, and creativity, think outside the box to solve the secrets of the room. The clock is ticking, and you’ve only got 60 minutes to prove your might and escape in time.

Game Rooms

We have 6 unique rooms to choose from,
each of them challenging, engaging, and fun!

Distracting Ball Pit

Riddles, puzzles, and balls! Lots of balls!

This room is purely designed for your inner-child.

Admit it, you secretly want to play in a gigantic ball pit, right? Well, you’re in luck, because this room is just that; an enormous ball pit for adults of all ages, full of puzzles!

Don’t let our balls distract you, because you’ve only got 60 minutes to escape from “Distracting Ball Pit”.

Gulag - ГУЛАГ

Attention Comrades!

You’ll not only have to rely on your own skill, but also the skills of your compatriots in the opposite cell in order to obtain your freedom.

Succeed, and you’ll escape permanent frozen exile.

Fail, and you’ll become the latest members of the glorious regime.

You’d better make it quick, because you’ve only got 60 minutes to escape from our Soviet “Gulag”.

Hotel Checkout

After a fantastic trip in Dublin, you realise that your flight home leaves in a few hours. The only problem is, you still haven’t checked out of your hotel room.

You quickly call down to reception, and they’ve advised that standard check-out time is in an hour.

To make things worse, you can’t remember where you’ve put the room key!

Now you’ve got 60 minutes to find the key, and proceed with your “Hotel Check-out”.

Leprechaun Gold

They say that a pot of leprechaun gold can be found at the end of a rainbow.

You know how difficult it is to get to the end of a rainbow, so you probably don’t have any leprechaun gold.

Well you’re in luck, because we’ve actually caught our very own leprechaun!

He’s got a full-time job as a shoemaker, and is currently away delivering shoes in the Midlands.

We’d like to invite you and your friends into our cottage to try and collect as much “Leprechaun Gold” as you possibly can before he gets back in 60 minutes and catches you.

Three Bears' House

Goldilocks isn’t here this time, so you’ve decided to rummage through the three Bears’ house yourself.

Unfortunately, they see you enter their house, and decide to lock you in!

You’ve been given an hour to escape before they come back with their friends to enjoy a tasty human meal.

Follow in Goldilocks’ footsteps through the “Three Bears’ House” to see if you can escape before they come back in 60 minutes.

Vaulted

Money is not the only thing that talks when you’re locked inside a bank vault.

With air supplies low, and security tight, your team will have only 60 minutes to escape.

If you fail, you’ll pay the ultimate price and become non-withdrawable!

Succeed, and your team will become “Vaulted” masters!

FAQs

– the GAME –

  • WHY SHOULD WE DO AN ESCAPE ROOM?
    LIVE ESCAPE GAMES ARE EXTREMELY FUN! THEY’LL CHALLENGE YOUR MIND, MAKE YOU THINK OUTSIDE THE BOX, DEVELOP TEAMWORK, AND GET YOUR BLOOD FLOWING! SOME EVEN CONSIDER THEM QUITE ADDICTIVE!
  • IS IT SCARY?
    ESCAPE ROOMS ARE ONLY SCARY IF YOU SUFFER FROM CLEITHROPHOBIA (FEAR OF BEING LOCKED IN AN ENCLOSED SPACE), OR A SEVERE CASE OF CLAUSTROPHOBIA (FEAR OF CONFINED PLACES).
    NOTHING WILL POP-OUT TO SCARE YOU, SO IF YOU’RE EASILY FRIGHTENED, YOU HAVE NOTHING TO WORRY ABOUT.
  • ARE WE BEING RECORDED?
    WE WILL HAVE CCTV IN ALL OF OUR ROOMS TO MONITOR THE GAMES.
    THIS IS FOR YOUR AND OUR PROTECTION, AND TO ENSURE BEST GAME EXPERIENCE POSSIBLE.
  • WHAT HAPPENS IF SOMEONE IN OUR GROUP WANTS TO LEAVE EARLY?
    THIS IS EXTREMELY RARE, BUT IF IT DOES OCCUR, THE OTHER PLAYERS MAY CHOOSE TO CONTINUE PLAYING OR NOT.
    PLAYERS THAT LEAVE EARLY MAY NOT BE ALLOWED BACK IN ONCE THEY’VE LEFT.
  • WHAT IF I NEED TO USE THE TOILET THOUGH?
    YOU WILL BE GIVEN A FINAL OPPORTUNITY TO USE THE TOILET FACILITIES PRIOR TO STARTING YOUR ESCAPE EXPERIENCE.
  • ARE HINTS GIVEN IF WE GET STUCK?
    YES, IF YOU NEED A HINT, YOU MAY ASK FOR THEM.
    WE MIGHT ALSO ASSIST IF WE NOTICE YOU’RE STRUGGLING TOO MUCH.
  • WHAT HAPPENS IF WE DAMAGE SOMETHING?
    PLEASE USE BEST JUDGEMENT.
    IF IT’S NOT WORKING, YOU’RE PROBABLY DOING SOMETHING WRONG.
    NOTHING NEEDS BRUTE FORCE.
    SHOULD YOU HAPPEN TO BREAK ANYTHING, YOU MAY BE REQUIRED TO PAY FOR THE DAMAGES.
    ADDITIONALLY, THEFT OF ANY OBJECTS WILL BE IMMEDIATELY REPORTED TO THE GARDA.
  • WHAT HAPPENS IF TIME RUNS OUT BEFORE WE SOLVE ALL PUZZLES?
    UNFORTUNATELY, THE GAME ENDS, AND YOU’VE FAILED TO ESCAPE IN TIME.
    REMEMBER: IT’S NOT ABOUT HOW FAST YOU CAN ESCAPE, IT’S ABOUT HOW MUCH FUN YOU HAVE IN THE PROCESS!

    DUE TO THE NATURE OF LIVE ESCAPE GAME EXPERIENCES, FLEXIBILITY ISN’T EASY.

    YOU WILL UNFORTUNATELY NOT HAVE SUFFICIENT TIME TO COMPLETE THE ROOM.
  • WE HAVE A BOOKING, BUT CAN NO LONGER VISIT ON THAT SPECIFIC DATE / TIME ANYMORE.

  • CAN WE JUST SHOW UP WITHOUT A BOOKING?

  • CAN WE BRING OUR CHILDREN?
